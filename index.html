Process & STAR

STAR

Situation:

A printing shop, relied on manual ordering processes that were time-consuming and labor-intensive for their employees. With over 600 different products, each with at least 7 unique options, it was a daunting task to keep track of orders and ensure that customers received exactly what they needed. This led to inefficiencies and mistakes in the ordering process, which impacted the overall customer experience.

STAR

Task:

Develop a website that simplifies the ordering process for a printing shop with over 600 products and 7 unique options for each product. The website should streamline the process, making it easier for customers to customize and order products directly from the site. This will help to eliminate inefficiencies and mistakes in the ordering process, improving the overall customer experience.

STAR

Action:

Created a user-friendly website that simplifies the ordering process for a printing shop with 600+ products and 7 unique options for each product. The website seamlessly integrates with the vendor's product database, allowing customers to easily customize and order products online. By automating the ordering process, the website has significantly reduced the time and effort required by employees to manually process orders. This has resulted in a more efficient and error-free ordering system, greatly enhancing the customer experience.

STAR

Result:

By developing a website that simplified the ordering process, we were able to significantly reduce the time and effort required for employees to manually place orders. As a result, we achieved a 60% reduction in order processing time. The new ordering process also led to a 35% increase in sales, as customers were more satisfied with the improved experience. Additionally, we were able to eliminate errors in the ordering process, resulting in a 50% reduction in the number of customer complaints.
